Virtual Bookshelf

I'm not yet proficient enough to do this myself, but with the power of search engines I can use other smarter people's code! Thanks to Petar Gyurov for the Github repo. My intent is to use this virtual bookshelf to keep track of how I like my books ordered. Sometimes you have to take 'em down to clean the shelf but this should make it easier to put them all back. Might take me a bit to finish updating this. I have too many books lol.
